Carne Asada Breakfast Burrito

Description

A wonderful way to change up ordinary breakfast burrito by taking it to a new level.

A little taste of mexico

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe tomato
  • 1 medium onion
  • 8 tortillas
  • 1 pound lean chicken breast
  • 1 red pepper

Steps

  1. Chop all the vegetables
  2. fry the chicken and set aside
  3. fry the vegetables and mix with the meat once soft
  4. place incredients in tortilla and wrap tightly
